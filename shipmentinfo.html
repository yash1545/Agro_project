Shipment Process

Understand how export shipment works after order confirmation.

1. Order Confirmation

Once payment is completed, the order is confirmed and prepared for shipment.

2. Packaging & Documentation

The farmer prepares the product and required export documents for shipment.

3. Pickup by Export Agent

An external export agent collects the goods from the farmer for international delivery.

4. In Transit

The shipment is transported to the buyer's country via international logistics.

5. Delivered to Buyer

The goods are delivered to the buyer and the shipment process is completed.
